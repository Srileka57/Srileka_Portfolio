Face Mists

Here are three great face mists suitable for different skin types: 1. **For Dry Skin: Caudalie Grape Water** - This mist is hydrating and soothing, enriched with organic grape water. It helps to provide moisture and refresh the skin without any sticky residue. 2. **For Oily/Acne-Prone Skin: Mario Badescu Facial Spray with Aloe, Herbs, and Rosewater** - This lightweight mist contains aloe, herbs, and rosewater, making it ideal for oily or acne-prone skin. It helps balance oil production and provides a refreshing boost. 3. **For Sensitive Skin: La Roche-Posay Thermal Spring Water** - Rich in minerals, this soothing mist is perfect for sensitive skin. It calms irritation and redness, making it a great option for those with reactive skin types. Each of these mists addresses specific needs while providing hydration and refreshment!
